On the distant world of Dorin, life clings to existence within sprawling, self-sustaining domed cities like Veridia. 

Shielded from the brutal, ever-expanding wrath of their dying sun, generations have lived in eternal night under the grim certainty that their star—once their lifeblood—is now poised to devour its own children. 

Young Kael Solan, a brilliant mind in Veridia, shoulders an impossible burden: he is part of Dorin's Last Hope, the scientists relentlessly toiling to decipher the universe's most complex secrets. 
Their desperate mission is to mend their dying star or guide their people to a new home among the indifferent cosmos. 

But as the sun's hunger grows, time is running out. 
The fate of an entire civilization rests on Kael and his fellow scientists. 

Can they unravel the mysteries of existence before Dorin becomes nothing more than a memory
