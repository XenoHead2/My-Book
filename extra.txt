Solan/Vira Family:

  The protagonist, a gifted anomaly living in secret, whose very existence threatens
   Veridia's perfect order
  
- Varyn Vira (Daughter of Preen): 
  Kael's mother, a brilliant, driven biologist whose research into Dorin's ancient past leads her to 
  the city's greatest secret.

   Vigilae unit and Kael's devoted confidante.

Sirana’s People
 * Feloc Lintal - Head of Theoretical Physics and Subatomic Engineering. 
He represents the high-level, theoretical skepticism of Sirana's scientific establishment. 
His job is to grasp the complex, non-local quantum mechanics behind the QEDs 
(Quantum Entanglement Devices) and approve them on a scientific basis. 
He is focused on the why and how of the entanglement process itself.
 * Daran - Senior Communications Engineer (Wizened, Aged Dorinian)
 He represents the practical, infrastructure side. 
His job is the massive undertaking of integrating the new quantum channels and signal
 strings into Sirana's existing, non-quantum Visiveiw Comms network without shutting the 
city down. 
He is focused on the hardware and integration.  
Lyra - Subatomic Theorist (Young Woman, Sharp-Eyed)
She represents the analytical and verification side. 
Her job is to scrutinize every data point and postulate that Conduit presents, ensuring the claims are plausible, and testing the principles of quantum mechanics on a smaller scale before integration. She is focused on the proof and testing.
