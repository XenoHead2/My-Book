---The World---

-Dorin: The alien world the story is set in.
-Leeta and Orin: The twin, highly visible moons in Dorin's sky.
-The Great Blight: A past catastrophic environmental and biological collapse that rendered Dorin's surface uninhabitable and necessitated the creation of the -Nanoweave Shield and the contained city of Veridia.
-Veridia: The only known city on Dorin, highly controlled and protected by the Nanoweave Shield and governed by a strict regime of order and efficiency.
-Nanoweave Shield: The massive, protective energy dome covering Veridia, essential for insulating the city from Dorin’s harsh environment and maintaining the atmosphere.
-Nanoweave Metals: A self-repairing, tessellated metallic compound used in the city's vital infrastructure, known for its exceptional strength and capacity to conduct and contain massive energy fields (e.g., the Nanoweave Shield).
-Hyperglass: A synthetic, crystalline material with unparalleled transparency and tensile strength, often layered with Nanoweave components for maximum defense and insulation from Dorin's harsh environment.
-Vidsplay  - Broadcast videos, entertainment, news, weather
-Sol: Currency on Dorin
-Visiveiw - Video and voice communication systems require cables to connect

---Places/Things---

-The Solarium:
The central, enclosed biological habitat within Veridia where Varyn researches on
Dorin's native flora and fauna were rescued during the Blight and before Dorin became unable to support life outside the dome.

-The Archives: 
The city’s central data repository, which uses a massive Hyper-Crystalline Matrix to render historical data and knowledge instantly and flawlessly, allowing for zero-latency retrieval.

-The Mag-Lev System: Veridia’s primary elevated transit network (Mark 4), which uses modulated magnetic fields for suspension, resulting in near-zero vibration and highly efficient, high-speed travel.

-The Anomaly: A persistent, unsettling flaw or data corruption of DNA detected by Varyns systems, which defies all traditional modeling and logic. 

-Dorin's Fauna: Indigenous but highly engineered life forms on Dorin, meticulously studied by Veridia's
Biologists often exhibit complex and unpredictable genetic coding.

* Rixian Scuttle-Spider: (Dust/Soil reinforcement)
* Auroran Vine-Lizard: (Photosynthetic carnivore)
* Biomass Grazier: (Meat supply/living factory)
* Aerofoil Couriers: (Avian Cohort/Sensor delivery)
* Felicis Aurum: (Companion Cohort/Living therapy unit)
  Fauna Cohorts: The official classification system used by Veridia's biologists (like Varyn) to categorize and track the different engineered life forms based on their primary function or habitat (e.g., Cohort 1 for biomass, Cohort 4 for resilience).

---Technology---
-Synth-Protein: A refined, hardened biological compound used in Veridia's engineered fauna (e.g., in the Rixian Scuttle-Spider's carapace), designed for maximum durability and low-energy maintenance.

-Hyper-Crystalline Matrix: A massive, sub-atomic crystalline lattice located beneath Veridia that serves as the central data storage medium. It allows for the instantaneous, zero-latency rendering of all historical and current information across the city's Projection Nodes.

-Cold Fusion / Cold Fission: The specialized, high-pressure, contained, clean-energy process used to instantaneously dismantle raw matter (fission) to a sub-atomic level and weave specific atoms into required molecular structures (fusion). The process is defined by its ability to achieve fusion without the high thermal input required by traditional methods.

-Chroniton Inducer: Zandil's powerful, salvaged machine designed to study and potentially manipulate the newly discovered properties of quantum entanglement.

-Quantum Entanglement: The principle where two particles become linked and share the same instantaneous state, regardless of the distance separating them.
QEO (Quantumly Entangled Objects): Microscopic particles used in Zandil’s research that are linked across space and time.

-Bismuth-Silicate Matrix: The specialized material required to stabilize and observe quantum entanglement in Zandil’s experimental chamber.
Polarity Flip: The instantaneous, controlled action taken in Zandil's lab to manipulate the quantum state of the paired particles.

---Mechanical Life---
Dorinian Mechanical Life Hierarchy:
-Vigilae: 
       Sentient robots, self-aware and independent, can hold positions of power
       Have equal rights as biologicals, treated as coequal citizens
       Can not be owned by any life form
       Nearly impossible to reprogram, usually a full wipe would be required
       Only the extremely skilled and talented, and maybe lucky, can do it.
       This is seen as murder on Dorin and is illegal except in extreme         circumstances.*
       Capable of: emotions, creativity, ambition, self-improvement, and motivation
       Can own either of the lesser mech styles but not another Vigilae.
       Examples: Rixie, Gus, Tridle
-Mechs: 
       Primitive AI, most are capable of speech and following complicated verbal             directives
       No self-determination
       Reprogrammed somewhat easily to do new tasks or serve new owners
       Considered property, but usually stays with one owner/manager/family
       No emotions, no feelings, no ambition/creativity/self-motivation
       Examples: Culinary Units, Auto Driving vehicles, autonomous equipment
-Drones:
       No AI, simple scripting drives them
       Can be programmed and reprogrammed very easily for different tasks
       Very rarely can drones speak
       Considered property and move between different owners/jobs/positions                     regularly
       They are sometimes even rented out and then returned
       Usually temporary helpers to both biological and Vigilae Dorinians
       Examples: Walking tool box, cleaning units, supply/storage units, door locks
